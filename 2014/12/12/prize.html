SIAG/LA Linear Algebra Prize

Dec 12, 2014 • David Bindel

The call for nominations for the next SIAG/LA prize has been posted. The prize is awared to authors of the most oustanding English-language paper in applicable linear algebra published in English in a peer-reviewed journal in the three calendar years preceding the year of the award. Nominations are due by February 15, 2015. The prize will be presented at the upcoming SIAM LA meeting.
