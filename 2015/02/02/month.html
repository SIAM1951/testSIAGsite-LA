SIAM Activity Group on Linear Algebra

A month in linear algebra

Feb 2, 2015 • David Bindel

The SIAM LA15 call for papers is available! Minisymposium proposals are due late March; abstracts and contributed presentations are due late April. Also, please send in your nominations for the SIAG/LA prize by Feb 15!

Several conferences of possible interest to SIAG-LA members were announced this month on the SIAG-LA digest and NA-digest, including:

  • Workshop on Optimization and Big Data in May 2015
  • PETSc 20th anniversary workshop in June 2015
  • CSCS-USI Summer School on Effective HPC in July 2015
  • Domain Decomposition Methods for PDEs in Aug 2015

A possibly interesting items from the web at large: Linear Algebra - Foundations to Frontiers is again being offered on the EdX platform by the good folks at UT Austin (started Jan 28).

New books!

  • Preconditioning and the Conjugate Gradient Method in the Context of Solving PDEs by Málek and Strakoš
  • Numerical Methods in Matrix Computations by Ake Bjork.
  • Matrix Algebra for Linear Models by Marvin H. J. Gruber.
  • Topics in Quaternion Linear Algebra by Leiba Rodman.
