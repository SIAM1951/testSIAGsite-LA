SIAM Activity Group on Linear Algebra

A month in linear algebra

Mar 14, 2015 • David Bindel

The post for this month is written on a plane to Salt Lake City; I hope to see many of you at SIAM CSE!

The SIAM LA15 call for papers is available! Minisymposium proposals are due late March; abstracts and contributed presentations are due late April.

The deadline for the CIME Summer School in June 2015 is coming fast; if you know students or postdocs who might be interested, please encourage them to apply!

Several conferences of possible interest to SIAG-LA members were announced this month on the SIAG-LA digest and NA-digest, including:

  • Paths, Pivots, and Practice: The Power of Optimization in June 2015
  • CIME Summer School on Exploiting Hidden Structure in Matrix Computations in June 2015
  • Sparse Days in June-July 2015
  • Domain Decomposition Methods in July 2015
  • Applied and Numerical Linear Algebra in July 2015
  • Graduate Student Modeling Workshop in July 2015
  • Modeling and Optimization: Theory and Applications in July 2015
  • ICERM Mathematics in Data Science in July 2015
  • Model Reduction of Parameterized Systems in October 2015
