Tenth SIAM Conference on Applied Linear Algebra

Monterey, California, October 26-29, 2009.
Organized by SIAG/LA.

Chen Grief has written a report (PDF, 36K) on the meeting.

The triennial SIAG/LA prize was awarded at this meeting to Zlatko Drmač and Krešimir Veselić for their paper “New Fast and Accurate Jacobi SVD Algorithm, Parts I-II,” SIAM Journal on Matrix Analysis and Applications, 29 (4), pp. 1322-1342 and 1343-1362 (2008).

As part of the meeting, a series of presentations were given on the History of Numerical Linear Algebra. The two-part minisymposium was organized by Ilse Ipsen and Sven Hammarling.

The Activity Group’s Business Meeting was held on October 27, 2009 at the conference.
