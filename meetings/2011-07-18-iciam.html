Minisymposia at 2011 International Congress on Industrial and Applied Mathematics (ICIAM 2011)

Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada, July 18-22, 2011.
Sponsored by SIAG/LA.

SIAG/LA organized a linear algebra “track” at the 2011 ICIAM. A “track” is a part of the program that is devoted to a particular field, in this case linear algebra. It consists of at least one invited topical speaker, four to six minisymposia, and contributed sessions spread out over two days.
